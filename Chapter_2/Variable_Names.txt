Variables are legal if:

1. A variable name can contain only numbers, letters, and underscores.
2. A variable name can’t start with a number.

The following are guidelines for legal variable names:

- Choose descriptive names
- Be consistent
- Follow the traditions of the language
- Keep the length in check

Illegal names:

- 1test
- test!@

Legal bad names:

- alpha7345690876
- thisisprobablynotaverygoodvariablenamebutyoucanseewhereimgoing

Legal good names:

- name
- age
- weight
